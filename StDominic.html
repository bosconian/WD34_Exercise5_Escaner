St. Dominic Cathedral

located in Bayombong, Nueva Vizcaya

If you are fascinated with religious sites, you have to lay your eyes on the Saint Dominic Cathedral. Found at Bayombong, Nueva Vizcaya, this premier tourist spot was previously named Saint Augustine Church. It envelopes a Baroque architecture and a striking facade that always leaves the devotees in awe. With an octagonal bell tower made out of red bricks, this church remains active today despite being ravished by extreme calamities such as earthquakes and fires.
